About Us

Our Story

Seven friends from diverse backgrounds meeting at one course at University of Glasgow—a tech enthusiast, analyst, engineer, and more—were exploring Glasgow when they became frustrated with the limited, high-impact transport options. They envisioned a new way to get around: app-based rentals of e-bikes and e-scooters, allowing easy, eco-friendly city travel. Excited by the idea, they pooled their skills and created "CHIME," a company committed to providing sustainable, hassle-free mobility options for Glasgow’s residents and visitors. Today, CHIME’s fleet of e-bikes and e-scooters empowers people to explore the city efficiently and sustainably, reducing congestion and emissions for a greener Glasgow.

Our Vision & Mission

Our vision to create a greener, more connected Glasgow by redefining urban mobility with sustainable, accessible e-bike and e-scooter rentals for everyone.

At CHIME, we aim to transform city travel by providing eco-friendly, convenient e-bikes and e-scooters that reduce congestion, lower emissions, and empower Glasgow’s residents and visitors to explore their city with ease and responsibility. We are committed to promoting a healthier, cleaner urban environment by offering a reliable and sustainable alternative to traditional transportation.
