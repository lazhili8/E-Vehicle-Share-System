Download the app or visit the website to get started. Log in to your account or sign up if you don't have one already. Select your location and choose your rental bike. When you're finished with your ride, simply return the bike and make your payment.

Simply scan the QR Code on a bike with the nextbike by CHIME app. The Frame Lock will open automatically.

RETURN

Simply locate an official station in your app to return your bike. Please note that e-bikes must be returned specifically to e-bike stations, which you can also find on the app map. Once you arrive, press the Frame Lock lever down until it beeps. Always return bikes to an official station to avoid a service fee.
